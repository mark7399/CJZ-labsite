Funded Projects¶

Our research group focuses on the research and application of cutting-edge methods for biomedical data analysis. We have led 3 national-level projects, 2 postdoctoral projects, and 1 general project from Guangdong Province, with a total funding of over 4.1 million RMB.

No. Project Type Project Name Funding Period
1 National Natural Science Foundation of China (General Program) Research on Spatial Multi-omics Data Generation and Integration Based on Graph Computing 500,000 RMB 2026-2029
2 National Key R&D Program of China Digital Twin Cell Technology for Subcellular-resolution Multi-omics Based on Generative Algorithms 2,941,100 RMB 2024-2027
3 National Natural Science Foundation of China (Youth Program) Early Diagnosis of Alzheimer's Disease Based on Brain Network Harmonics 300,000 RMB 2022-2024
4 Postdoctoral Special Grant (Station-based) Project (16th batch) Research on Multi-scale Brain Network Wavelet Analysis Theory and Application for Brain Diseases 180,000 RMB 2023-2024
5 Postdoctoral General Grant Project (69th batch) Early Diagnosis of Alzheimer's Disease Based on Multi-source Heterogeneous Big Data 80,000 RMB 2021-2023
6 Guangdong Natural Science Foundation (General Program) Research and Application of Fusion Methods for Multi-source Heterogeneous Biomedical Big Data 100,000 RMB 2022-2024
